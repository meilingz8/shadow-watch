## рҹ‘Ғ What Is ShadowWatch?

ShadowWatch is a **Chrome browser extension** that intercepts every network request your browser makes and tells you вҖ” in plain English вҖ” exactly which companies are collecting your data as you browse.

Not technical jargon. Not IP addresses. **Names.**

> *"Facebook Pixel is tracking your purchases right now."*
> *"TikTok's surveillance pixel just loaded on this news site."*
> *"This page uses Canvas fingerprinting вҖ” a tracking method that survives clearing your cookies."*
